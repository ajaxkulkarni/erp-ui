How To Use

HR Easy is an online employee management tool which is developed to enable small and medium scale growing business to manage their resources in smooth and easy way.

Activate your account

Once your account is activated by our team, please login to HREasy using the password received in the mail.

Then change your password to something you feel comfortable with.


Company Information

After this, a company information screen will be opened. All the company information can be changed later on EXCEPT Company Name. Fill in all the company information required on this screen.


Dashboard

Here, you will see an admin dashboard. It currently has following 4 options- Employee section, Leaves section, Salary section and company information which you just filled.


Employee section

Employee section has the following sub sections -

Add Employee

Here, you can add all the employees in your company, along with their personal, educational, and professional and bank details. Please note that bank details are mandatory in order to process employee salary.

View Employees

Here, you can find list of employees you entered and you can Edit/View any particular employee. View employee option also contains a feature of printing employee information in PDF format.


Leaves section

Leaves section is designed to manage all employee leaves. It will consist of -

Leave settings

This is very important aspect for your company as you CANNOT proceed to adding leaves unless to define a leave structure for your company from this settings. Company leave structure is the no. of sick leaves or the no. of casual leaves the company has allotted its employees on yearly/ monthly basis. If you don't want to use any particular leave type, you can put 0 against it.

Add leaves

Here, you can add leave for particular employee PROVIDED you have added at least one employee from add employee section and defined your leave setting from leave settings section. Also if an employee is newly added to the system, you need to provide an opening balance for that employee, otherwise all leaves balance will be considered as default 0 for that employee. You need to provide info like from and to date of leave, no of days, reason, type of leave i.e. sick or casual or personal etc.

Note: If the leave balance after applying the leave tends to 0, a warning will be given saying that leave limit is exceeding. If you chose to proceed in this case, the leaves will be considered for without pay.

View leaves

In this section, you can view the no of leaves taken summary for all the employees. You can view/edit details of any particular employee both yearly and month-wise.

When you view leaves for particular employee, you can also cancel/reject any leave taken by that employee. After cancellation, leave balance will be reverted accordingly.

On this screen, there is another important section, which shows the leave balance of that employee. If you chose to edit it, you can edit the leave balance of that employee, which you will need to do after adding a new employee to the system to provide some opening balance.


Salary section

This is the payroll/ salary management section of the application. It consists of -

Salary settings

This is another important aspect as it defines the salary structure i.e. the general division of salary in your company. E.g. The basic pay %, the type of benefits, type of deductions, allowances etc. You can define it either in amount or in percentage.

Employee salary

After deciding the salary structure, you can go ahead and add employee salaries from this section. It shows you the default breakdown of the employee salary as per your company salary structure. You can choose to edit it or save it as it is.

Salary details

This is the section where you can see salaries of all the employees and also get their pay slips for respective months. For the ongoing month, changes in any employee salary structure will immediately reflected.

Note: For previous months, all the salary slips are automatically stored at the end of each month which will be available only for view/download purpose.
