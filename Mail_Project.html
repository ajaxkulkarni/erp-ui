Mail Settings

E-mail notofications can be sent to your registered mail IDs.
Select the checkbox in the table below to tell us 'When do you wish to receive an email notifiction?'

# My Options
1 I wish to receive a notification for every project record upadate.
Email will be sent whenever anyone adds/modifies/deletes any record in the project. This includes comments, file uploads for all records.
2 I wish to receive a notification whenever a relevent record is upadate.
Email will be sent whenever anyone adds/modifies/deletes any record which is either created by you or is asigned to you. This includes comments, file uploads as well.
3 I do not wish to receive a notification for project upadate.
Email will not be sent for any change in project records.
